
#Scaffold database with EFC
Scaffold-DbContext 'Server=localhost\sqlexpress; Database=BookStoreDb; Trusted_Connection=True; MultipleActiveResultSets=true; TrustServerCertificate=True' Microsoft.EntityFrameworkCore.SqlServer -ContextDir Data -OutputDir Data

Provider: Microsoft.EntityFrameworkCore.SqlServer
ContextDir: where to place the DbContext class
OutputDir: where to place the entity classes
